ABOUT US

The Charcoal Grill was borne out of a vision to deliver
a unique experience to the Nigerian meat-Lover. The
restaurant was designed to ensure that diners have a
relaxed, yet modern and upscale experience at the
establishment. After Months of planning, The
restaurant was launched in November 2013.


Since launched, the restaurant has developed a
clientele and has evolved its offerings and operations
to consistently deliver best-in-class services to Our
Guests
